How it Works?

1.Purchase a Pitching Ticket.

An important quality of any entrepreneur is the ability and willingness to fundraise and take calculated risks. We ask studentpreneurs to demonstrate this quality by sourcing RWF 10,000 and purchasing a pitching ticket to qualify for entry. If you are working on teams, let the team leader be the one to make the purchase.

  • Pitching tickets are only available electronically.
  • Every ticket grants you an automatic entry into the competition.
2. Submit an Entry.

Once you have purchased your pitching ticket, our team will reach out with an e-form where you will submit your project idea and upload supporting materials such as business plans, decks and videos. Please note that we only accept for-profit ventures at this moment.

Though we want you to be brief, we encourage you to submit every material that will help us assess the potential and viability of your business idea.

3. Pitch To our Campus VCs.

This our most awaited moment. Once the Campus VC committee has reviewed all the submitted materials, you will be given an opportunity to present your idea to a panel of judges composed of investors, students and independent consultants.

  • At Campus level, every pitch runs for 5 minutes
  • Every pitch is followed by a Q&A session of 5 minutes.
  • Judges select the top 5 studentpreneurs who proceed to the grand finale.

And Finally...

4. WIN.

If you've made it this far, congratulations. The top 5 will compete in the monthly Campus VC Battlefield to crown one winner. The winner walks away with 500,000 RWF with a guarantee of follow - on support.

  • The grand finales are held every month.
  • The winner gets featured in mainstream media for heightened exposure
  • The winner gets facilitated warm introductions to partner investors.
